About Us

Shubham Transport Corporation (STC) is a trusted name in the Indian logistics sector, offering efficient and reliable container and cargo transportation services since 2007. Founded by Mr. Pawan Agarwal, STC provides pan‑India transportation services operating out of Mumbai and Hyderabad. The company maintains strong operational presence at major ports JNPT and BPT.
